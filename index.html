About Nadia

My name is Andrey, and Nadia is my mother (her name means "Hope"). She emigrated to America over 20 years ago with my siblings and me as refugees of war. I believe my mother has a God-given talent for poetry that has been hidden far too long in the notes section of her phone. So I created this website and translated her poems into English so more people can experience her beautiful, prayer-like poetry. She is the most kind, selfless, and loving person I know—always putting God and family first. I hope you find as much joy in her words as I found in bringing them to the world. Thank you for being here.
